Mission Statement

The new website's goal is to increase lead generation by 50%, while also maintaing Training Zone's existing customer base

Success Evaluation

Tracking the increase in sales contacts generated by the website is crutial to proving the effectivness of the website.

To this end, a HTML 5 Webform will capture all requests for contact generated by the website. All requests will be loged for recordkeeping before being emailed to the client for actioning.

Target Audience

Training Zone has identified the target audience as 28-30, Mature Age job-seekers.

The mature Age Jobseeker is not a digital native. As a result, the new website will be designed for maximumm ease of access.

For example, Research has shown Menus that feature an icon instead of text increase ambiguity and confuse the non-digital natives. The notorious 'hamburger icon' will not be making an appearance.

Flexiable layouts will be used to ensure the site can be accessed from a multitude of devices.
